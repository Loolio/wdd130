Lodwar, Turkana County, Kenya

Lodwar, the capital of Turkana County in northwestern Kenya, is a culturally rich and scenic town nestled between the Kalawasee and Turkwel Rivers. Known for its vibrant Turkana heritage, historical landmarks like Kenyatta House, and proximity to Lake Turkana, it offers visitors a unique blend of tradition, history, and adventure. With its desert landscapes, bustling markets, and growing infrastructure, Lodwar serves as both a cultural hub and a gateway to exploring the wider Turkana region, the Cradle of Mankind!
