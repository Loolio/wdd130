Hello! I am Loolio. I wanted to take this opportunity to introduce myself to all of you.

I am glad that I would have the chance to study programming and I am currently enrolled in two courses this semester: CSE 110 Programming Building Blocks and WDD 130 Web Fundamentals to build in my pursuit to study a bachelors degree in Applied Technology.

I look forward to interacting with you this semester!
